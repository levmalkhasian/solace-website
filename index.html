How it works

Built to keep you honest.

🔒

NFC-gated focus

Tap your Solace tag to start a focus session. The only way to end it is to tap again — so quitting becomes a deliberate choice.

⚡

Real distraction blocks

Powered by Apple’s Screen Time APIs to block apps, categories, websites, and in-app purchases at the OS level.

⏰

Alarms you can’t snooze

Set a wake-up alarm that can only be silenced by scanning your tag. Available on iOS 26 and later.

📊

Focus stats

Track your time focused today, this week, this month, and for life. See what you’re reclaiming from distraction.

🛡

Privacy by default

Solace runs entirely on your device. No accounts, no trackers, no analytics. Your focus history never leaves your phone.

🚨

Emergency unblocks

Life happens. Solace gives you a small pool of emergency unblocks so you’re never truly locked out when you need to be free.
